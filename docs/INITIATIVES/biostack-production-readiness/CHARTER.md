# Initiative Charter

## Mission

Prove BioStack can accept free, paid, and provider users safely and operably in production, with truthful packaging, governed customer data, recoverable operations, and evidence-backed release gates.

## In scope

Frontend UX and tests; API/auth/consent; monthly Stripe lifecycle; provider intake; database migration/backup/restore; CI/deployment; probes, monitoring and rollback; legal/privacy/security; accessibility, SEO and support.

## Out of scope

Annual billing, new clinical/prescriptive features, unsupported AI claims, unrelated protocol-operations expansion, and production mutation without release authorization.

## Success criteria

Every blocking scenario passes in its required environment; security and release gates pass; human approvals are recorded; rollback is rehearsed; evidence is indexed; the release owner records GO.

## Stop conditions

Stop on missing product decisions, contract drift, unclear security ownership, unapproved customer-facing policy, missing live credentials/configuration, or evidence that cannot be reproduced safely.
