**See the interactions your stack is creating — before they become a problem.**

Select any combination of your active compounds and BioStack surfaces detected pathway overlaps, interaction flags, and evidence confidence scores. Name and date discrete protocol periods — Loading Phase, Maintenance, Off-Cycle, Washout — and anchor your timeline to them. When you're reviewing data, you're not looking at a flat stream of events. You're looking at structured phases with clear boundaries. The context makes the data legible.

Convert between mg, mcg, IU, and g with accuracy that matters when precision matters. Whether you're reconciling a supplier's label, verifying a dosing calculation, or translating a published study into your protocol, BioStack's unit conversion tools eliminate ambiguity from the math.
